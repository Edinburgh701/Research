More on Research

Below are relevant work/leadership experiences, and the **research** my mentors are preparing me to work with.

Mentorships

I am currently working with my graduate mentors in the Physics and Mathematics department to develop a deep understanding of the role and theory of symmetry in classical Mechanics. Topics include: Lagrangian & Hamiltonian dynamics, the study of manifolds/point set topology, writing mathematical proofs, group theory, elementary Lie groups,calculus of variations, Cartan calculus, and symplectic geometry (high focus). Our mentorship program includes supervised reading of the following texts: "Mathematical Methods of Classical Mechanics" (V.I. Arnold), "Introduction to Mechanics and Symmetry" (J.E. Marsden & T.S. Ratiu), "Foundations of Mechanics" (R. Abraham & J.E. Marsden).

Summer Research (2025)
